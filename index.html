Based on typical industry patterns (e.g. average export behaviour) rather than your own meter data — a reasonable estimate, not a measurement.

This tool never links to installers and earns nothing from what you buy. These are typical industry figures, not a quote — get 2–3 real quotes before deciding.

How this works: each upgrade's cost and annual saving are estimated from the assumptions below, using typical solar yield for your state. Payback = cost ÷ annual saving. Upgrades are ranked fastest-payback first, filled against your budget in order. Anything past the delay threshold is flagged rather than ranked, regardless of budget.

Not yet included: EV charging, insulation, double glazing. These are on the roadmap — this is an early working version covering the three upgrades that interact most: solar, battery, and hot water.

This tool models general assumptions against the numbers you enter — it does not consider your complete personal or financial circumstances, and nothing on this page is personal or general financial advice. Costs, rebates and STC prices vary — get 2–3 real quotes before deciding.
