How this works: each upgrade's cost and annual saving are estimated from the assumptions on the left, using typical solar yield for your state. Payback = cost ÷ annual saving. Upgrades are ranked fastest-payback first, filled against your budget in order. Anything past the delay threshold is flagged rather than ranked, regardless of budget.
